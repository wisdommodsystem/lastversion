About Us

Welcome to our platform, created by Apollo Belvedere (Mazigh Apollo) and initiated by Wisdom Circle â­•.

This website was established to document the number of non-religious individuals in Morocco, not just as numbers, but as a voice for everyone who feels part of this diverse community. Our aim is to show that we are not a silent minority, but a group of individuals who think freely and seek understanding and respectful dialogue.

Our mission goes beyond statistics; we strive to build a cohesive non-religious community in Morocco, a space where everyone feels included, safe, and respected. We envision this platform as a hub for communication, expression, and inspiration, where anyone can share their thoughts and experiences with confidence.

We believe that data tells our story, and knowledge opens doors for change. This project is just the beginning of our journey toward an aware community, where every voice is heard and every experience valued.

Join us, and be part of a movement toward awareness, dialogue, and positive change.
